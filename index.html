Sermon Notes
GOD WITH US: NOW WHAT?
Luke 2:25–35
PT 1 — WHEN WE RECEIVE JESUS, OUR WAITING TURNS INTO WORSHIP
Waiting doesn’t always end when Jesus arrives — it changes. Trust replaces tension.
Where in your life are you still waiting on God?
Has your waiting drawn you closer to God — or pushed you toward discouragement?
PT 2 — WHEN WE RECEIVE JESUS, OUR LIVES ARE REFOCUSED
Jesus is not an addition to our lives — He becomes the center.
What currently holds the most focus in your life?
In what areas might Jesus be calling you to realign your priorities?
FINAL REFLECTION
God is with us — not just at Christmas, but in the ordinary days that follow.
What is one intentional step you can take this week to live as someone who has truly received Jesus?
