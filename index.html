When It All Falls Apart: The Shattered Life
Job 1–2; Job 13:15
PT 1 — WHEN EVERYTHING FALLS APART AT ONCE (Job 1)
Some losses don’t come one at a time — they pile up. And when life collapses quickly, the hardest part isn’t only the pain, but the shock.
What part of your life feels like it fell apart all at once?
When things began unraveling, what did you want God to do immediately?
What would honest faith look like here — without pretending everything is fine?
PT 2 — WHAT DOES THE BIBLE SAY ABOUT THIS? (John 16:33; 2 Corinthians 4:8; Job 2:10)
Scripture does not deny suffering. It names it — and then anchors us in truth that pain is not the end of the story.
Which scripture from today speaks most directly to what you’re facing?
Where are you tempted to believe your suffering means God is distant?
What is the difference between accepting reality and losing hope?
PT 3 — FAITH THAT LOOKS LIKE JESUS
Faith that looks like Jesus does not avoid pain — it trusts the Father through it. Jesus shows us surrender without despair.
What would faith that looks like Jesus look like in your life right now?
Where is God asking you to keep trusting even without answers?
“Though He slay me, yet will I trust Him” (Job 13:15). What does that kind of trust mean for you?
FINAL REFLECTION
Faith is not pretending you are okay. Faith is choosing not to let brokenness have the final word.
What is one honest prayer you need to pray today?
